About Me

Welcome to my professional portfolio! I am a dedicated IB Diploma Programme Physics teacher at Oberoi International School with over 10 years of experience in physics education. My teaching philosophy centers around making complex physics concepts accessible and engaging for all students.

I hold a Master's degree in Physics from the University of Cambridge and a Postgraduate Certificate in Education. My research interests include innovative teaching methodologies in physics and the integration of technology in science education.

Teaching Philosophy

Inquiry-Based Learning

I believe in fostering curiosity through guided inquiry, allowing students to discover physics principles through exploration and experimentation.

Collaborative Approach

Physics is best learned through collaboration. I create opportunities for students to work together, share ideas, and learn from each other.

Real-World Applications

I connect theoretical concepts to real-world applications, helping students understand the relevance of physics in their everyday lives and future careers.
